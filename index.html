Welcome to Fish market: explore trading

Some people, like Petal, make money by trading.

They buy something at a price.

Then they sell it at a better price.

You are going to trade.

You will buy pet fish.

To make money, you will sell them.

If you make good trades, your success will grow. You will be able to trade at more markets.

If you trade poorly, your success will fall and you'll lose some markets.

If you want to check the price you bought or sold a fish at, select 'H' next to the fish.

Your ultimate goal is to buy a fizzer.

You need to make enough money before you can do this.

Try to find the trader who will sell you the fizzer.

Get ready to meet the other traders and start trading online!
